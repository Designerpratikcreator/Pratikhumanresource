About Us

Empowering Nepal Through Professional Human Resource Services

Pratik Human Resource is a modern employment and recruitment company dedicated to connecting talented individuals with the best companies in Nepal. We specialize in providing skilled professionals to industries such as healthcare, engineering, hospitality, design, management, administration, and technology.

Our mission is to create career opportunities for individuals while helping organizations build stronger teams. With advanced recruitment strategies, professional consultation, and client-oriented services, we ensure quality, trust, and long-term professional success.

We believe in innovation, professionalism, and ethical recruitment processes that uplift businesses and communities throughout Nepal.
